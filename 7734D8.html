Bridgerton and Twilight Have The Same Character Problem
2024-04-27

Bridgerton intertwines Regency-era romance with modern discussions of character complexity and potentially harmful tropes, paralleling issues seen in earlier media like "Twilight." The show delves into the intricate dynamics between Daphne and Simon, exploring their fraught relationship amid societal pressures and personal traumas. As the story unfolds, it challenges viewers to scrutinize the romanticization of problematic behaviors.
