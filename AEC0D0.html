Don't worry if you have mice at home. Here's a simple way to kill them one by one, safe and non-toxic
2023-08-28

DIY Mouse Trap: 1. Cut three sides of the top part of a plastic bottle, leaving one side uncut. 2. Punch symmetrical holes on the cut part of the bottle. 3. Insert disposable chopsticks into the holes. 4. Put rubber bands on the inserted chopsticks and mark a certain position with elasticity. 5. Punch two more symmetrical holes on the uncut side of the bottle. 6. Put rubber bands on the inserted chopsticks again.

7. Make a small hole on the bottom of the uncut side of the bottle. 8. Tie one end of a rope to the bottle's head. 9. Pull the rope backward to raise the bottle head and make a loop on the other end of the rope. 10. Insert a straightened paperclip into the loop and fix the food bait. 11. Insert the paperclip with the bait partially out through the small hole at the bottom of the bottle. 12. Attach the rope to the paperclip.
