Silicon Valley Still Reigns as America's Startup Haven
2023-07-20

Uber Early Investor: Silicon Valley Remains the Holy Land of Entrepreneurship Bill Gurley, who made a fortune by betting on Uber in its early days, believes that Silicon Valley is still the top choice for anyone looking to start a tech company. Despite now living in Austin, Texas, Gurley is adamant that the California region has not lost its edge.

The debate over whether Silicon Valley is still the epicenter of American entrepreneurship has been ongoing since the outbreak of the pandemic.
