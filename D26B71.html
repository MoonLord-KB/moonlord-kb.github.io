Google co-founder Sergey Brin is returning to the company to focus on a new AI system
2023-07-20

Google co-founder Sergey Brin has returned to the company to focus on the development of a new AI system, according to The Wall Street Journal. Brin has been working at Google's Mountain View office in California for three to four days a week, collaborating with research scientists on the creation of Google's next big AI system. He has been involved in discussions about technical issues, such as the "loss curve" method for measuring the performance of AI programs over time.
