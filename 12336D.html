Unveiling the Shadows: Unearthing a Crucial Breaking Bad Moment through a Neglected Better Call Saul Special
2023-08-16

AMC released a digital comic that bridges the gap between "Breaking Bad" and "Better Call Saul," showing how things could have been different. The comic, titled "Better Call Saul: Client Development," takes place during a crucial moment in "Breaking Bad" and follows Saul Goodman and Mike Ehrmantraut's perspective. It reveals that Saul may have considered having Walter White killed before their partnership began. The comic provides insight into Saul's mindset and showcases Mike's ingenuity. The events in the comic are mostly exclusive to the book, but it hints that there may be more stories to explore in the future.
